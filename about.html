General Awareness

We can no more ignore the severity of the situation. It is high time that we start taking care of yourselves and your loved ones.

Wear a mask.

Masks reduces the risk of the infected droplets entering your nostrils or mouth.

Sanitise your hands regularly.

Incasee your hands come in cantact with infected droplets, sanitising your hands with alcohol based sanitizer might disinfect them.

Stay Home

Avoid uncessarily going outdoors. This is the safest option.

Social Distancing

Distance between two people while talking may reduce the chances of exchange of infected droplets between them.

Know the Standard range of health parameters

At this stage, it is a must for everyone to be aware about the standard health parameters.

Normal Arterial Oxygen

75 mm Hg

Normal Body Temperature

97 °F
