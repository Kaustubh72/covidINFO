Why Maintaining a distance and wearing a mask is important?

The virus spreads mainly when respiratory droplets of an infected person (generated through coughing, sneezing, talking, singing) get into the mouth, nose or eyes of people who are nearby. Masks reduce the risk of these droplets entering your nostrils or mouth during a conversation and maintaing a distance even further lower the risk.

Why Sanitising your belongings is important?

People may also become infected by touching their mouth, nose or eyes after touching surfaces contaminated with the virus. The virus may survive on surfaces for a few hours up to several days.Thus it is important that these surroundings are sanitised timely as disinfectants are efficient in killing the virus.

Our Fight Against COVID 19

COVID 19 has taken the World by storm. In such a situation, it is the individuals themselves who can keep themselves safe.

Wear a mask.
